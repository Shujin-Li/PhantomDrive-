## Important note

The included countdown WAV files are project-ready 8-bit/arcade-style WAV files prepared for PhantomDrive's current file-name convention:

- countdown_three.wav
- countdown_two.wav
- countdown_one.wav
- countdown_go.wav
- countdown_full_321_go.wav

They are designed to match the selected Mixkit style: short, bright, energetic, arcade-racing, and not a low male voice.

## Other power-up SFX

The power-up WAV files are retained from the previous PhantomDrive sound pack and are intended as differentiated effects for boost/shield/EMP/repair/oil/magnet/custom/missile/invisibility/teleport/generic pickup events.
